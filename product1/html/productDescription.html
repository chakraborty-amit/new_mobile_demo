Group Gratuity Plan



Product Description


A group insurance plan that provides a lump sum to a beneficiary in case of death of a covered member during the defined covered period. A group term life policy is usually issued for a period of one year, and renewable each year.
The premiums are experienced rated, based on the company's deaths, and range of employee's ages.
